Connor Shipway is a developer, designer, animator, and multidisciplinary creative generalist based out of Raleigh, North Carolina.

For a while, he worked for a Raleigh-based VR company as a technical designer, finding and implementing clever solutions to develop effective XR experiences, such as HazVR and FirstModulAR.

Now, he works as a software engineer for a Durham-based finance company.

In his free time, he enjoys working on his own personal game development projects, such as the online action-adventure RPG Wild Yonder.
