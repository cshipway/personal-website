Connor Shipway is a designer, developer, animator, and multidisciplinary creative generalist based out of beautiful Sylva, North Carolina.

For the past 4 years, he worked for the Raleigh-based NextGen Interactions as a technical designer, finding and implementing clever solutions to develop effective XR experiences, such as HazVR and FirstModulAR. Now, he is on the lookout for new opportunities and his next big challenge.

In his free time, he enjoys working on his own personal game development projects, such as the online action-adventure RPG Wild Yonder.
